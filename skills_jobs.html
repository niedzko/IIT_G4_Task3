Roles

A major part of LendIt community's success will be due to the involvement of other roles that can make the application stand out from the rest. As we progress from the beginning to the end, here are the roles we will be incorporating.

Lead Developer

In our search for a lead developer, we are looking for someone who possesses outstanding skills and capabilities, as well as being able to work effectively with other leading teams. As part of their role, they will be responsible for the following:

  • Developing a close relationship with senior stakeholders to understand their business requirements and translate them into technical requirements for the development team based on the business requirements
  • Designing features or systems in accordance with technical specifications and documenting them
  • Assisting in the design, development and configuration of application needed to meet the requirements of business processes and applications
  • Being responsible for leading the development team in designing, developing, coding, testing, and debugging applications

Technical Designer

As part of our efforts, we are also looking for a Technical Designer with outstanding skills to be a part of the team. They will be responsible for analysing design ideas to see if they are feasible, working with designers, manufacturers, and vendors to ensure that the best products are produced, and using their skills and ability to ensure the best results can be achieved. The candidate for this position should possess a strong deadline-driven nature, have an aptitude for details, and be able to demonstrate solid software knowledge. A Technical Designer will have the following responsibilities as part of their role:

  • Ensure that the product specifications are feasible by working with designers, manufacturers, vendors, and clients to ensure that the specifications are feasible.
  • Assisting in the analysis of designs and determining which resources and timelines are required
  • As the need arises, implement any necessary design changes, and make contingency plans accordingly
  • Managing a team of technical and design professionals

UI/UX Designer

It is vital to have a UI/UX Designer involved in the development process of mobile applications. Therefore, we are seeking to find an experienced UI/UX Designer to make our product easier to use, more intuitive and more attractive. In fact, their purpose is to make sure that everything within the application is configured to facilitate the best user experience and, therefore, a higher level of engagement with the application. It is also important to point out that the UI/UX will also carry out the following responsibilities:

  • Collaboration between product managers and engineers is essential to gathering and evaluating users' requirements
  • Create concept maps, process flows, and storyboards to help illustrate design ideas
  • Create UI mockups and prototypes that visually illustrate how the application should look and function.
  • Develop a rough draft of the report and present it to key stakeholders and internal teams

Mobile App Developers

Additionally, we will be looking for mobile application developers to join our team. Our responsibility will be to ensure that our application will be compatible with all mobile platform, including iOS, Android, and any other route that crosses platform, so in order to accomplish this, we will need to find a developer with the necessary skills and experience who can make our application compatible with all mobile platforms. A role like this entails the following responsibilities:

Business Analyst

It is also important that we have a Business Analyst on board. At the project's initial stage, we will need a business analysis to help us understand what we need to do to succeed. In addition to analysing the business requirement, they will also help set the right goals so that the approach to development is the most efficient for the unique requirements for our application. As far as we are concerned, Business Analysts are one of the most crucial components of our mobile application development process. They will be responsible for ensuring the project's business value is kept in mind, always analysing the process, and helping everyone involved achieve the goals discussed at the start of the project.

QA Engineer

Having a QA Engineer in the team is essential to us as they can help us with any situation that may arise as we are developing the application as they can assist us in solving any issues we may encounter along the way. This means the QA Engineer is responsible for checking that the application contains no bugs and can run on a variety of devices, so that the application meets the needs of the intended users.
