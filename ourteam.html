Group Process

During assignment 2 we worked effectively as a group to use our bi-weekly meetings to equally distribute the work between ourselves. Throughout the assignment we also had clear and open communication, e.g. in our first meeting when we initially met as a team, both Vincent and Jason were open to say they would prefer not to chair the group but were happy to contribute to the weekly meetings and get on with the work. This clear communication within the group has let us progress with defined roles / personalities within the group.

With this in mind, one thing that has surprised us as a group is how easily we have got on and been able to work together without clashes. On reflection, the clear communication in the group has aided this as people have been open and honest and delivered work agreed to and if someone has a questions, is struggling or does not have the capacity to complete assigned work this has been communicated so other team members can assist in a timely manner. Opposed to say, a team member dropping off the face of the earth and the remaining members are not sure if they are continuing with their work or not.

One aspect we are hoping to change / improve on for this assignment 3 is we would aim to have work completed at an earlier date, so we have more time to evaluate and fine tune our work before submitting the assignment.
